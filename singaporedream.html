About Project

An in-depth interaction analysis done for my Principles of Interaction Design module Assignment 3 in DigiPen Year 1, Trimester 1. The brief is to pick any contemporary (published on or after 2010) interactive project (video game, board/tabletop/card/dice, app, other) and write an analysis paper, focusing on any aspect(s) of the project. For which I chose to write an analysis on The Singaporean Dream: The New Normal card game, focusing on the aspects of the Mechanics, Goals, and Dynamics. The assignment is evaluated based on the following criterias: Depth (20 Marks), Terminology (20 Marks), Engagement (20 Marks), Presentation (20 Marks), and Value (20 Marks).

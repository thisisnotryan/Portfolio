Hi! I'm Ryan Lim

Interaction Designer Game Designer Game Programmer

I’m Ryan Lim, an undergraduate at DigiPen Institute of Technology Singapore pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in User Experience and Game Design.

With a strong passion for UI/UX design, I specialize in crafting user-centric experiences and intuitive interfaces for web and mobile applications. My foundation was built during my time at Nanyang Polytechnic, where I earned a diploma in Interaction Design. I’m now focused on applying these skills to create engaging and seamless game designs, blending functionality with creativity in the world of game development.

As a passionate gamer, I’m driven by the opportunity to contribute to the gaming industry by designing innovative UI/UX solutions that enhance gameplay and push the boundaries of interactive experiences.

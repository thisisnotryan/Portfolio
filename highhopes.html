High Hopes

About Project

High Hopes is a Endless Rogue-like Jumper game fully designed and coded with unity by my team LiteWorks. A brief synopsis of the game is that your dog has been taken away by God, and your only choice to save your dog is to jump!. The core gameplay features includes "Obtaining the highest score possible, collecting milestone upgrades to aid your traversal, avoiding obstacles, and to collect power-ups to boost your score. Our team strives to achieve a game that is simple as we firmly believe in the phrase "Less is more" and we absolutely do not want to overscope for our project, hence we rather focus on the replay value of the game and to make use of the competetive spirit of gamers as they strive to be in the top leaderboards of the game and compete against their friends.

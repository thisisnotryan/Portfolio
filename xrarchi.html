XRArchi App

About Project

This is a group project for my Design Process module Assignment 3 in DigiPen Year 1, Trimester 1. Each group was tasked to decide on a style of architecture and my group chose Romaneseque and we ultimately decided to design a walkthrough gallery app targeted towards Architecture students for them to learn and navigate through various Romaneseque Buildings and feast on information of the architecture structures and origin.

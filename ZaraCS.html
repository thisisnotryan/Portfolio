Introduction

This is a Digial Product Enhancement Plan project dedicated to defining solutions using user research methods to refine, enhance, and improve the user experience of ZARA’s website

For this Enhancement Plan, I used certain strategies to define the solutions that I would be making which are the following in sequence:
1) I Conducted a Survey Research to collect Data.
2) I Conducted a Heuristic Evaluation on the Zara Website.
3) I Created 3 User Personas and define their user stories, journey and emphathy maps.
4) Finally, I showcased my concept and Ideas for the revamped website and showed why it works.

Survey Research

The purpose of this survey research is for me to fairly guage the experienced of Zara's visitors or if they are new to Zara, from this, I can also find out exactly which parts of the Zara's website users find the hardest to navigate as this allows me to look deeper into these parts to potentially make any enhancements or changes.

Through this survey, I was also able to get data by asking my survey doers to rate the visual design of Zara's Website to know how do Zara's Customers feel about the visual design of the website.

For users that have no prior experience with Zara or have not known about Zara, I provided them with some images of the Zara's Website and asked them to provide their truthful opinion based on the images provided.

Heuristic Evaluation

For the Heuristic Evaluation, I used Jakob's Neilsen's 10 principles and I have identifed that the primary issue of violation of Jakob's Nielsen's principles is regarding the aesthetic design, particularly in terms of readability and overall layout. From these evaluations I knew that refining the User Interface layout could effectively address this issue and enhance the website's usability.


For Jakob's Nelsen's 8th Princile which is Aesthetic and minimalist design, the general rule of thumb is that Interfaces should not contain information that is irrelevant or rarely needed. Every extra unit of information in an interface competes with the relevant units of information and diminishes their relative visibility.

Final Concept

After gathering feedback and suggestions from my survey and research, along with analyzing Jakob Neilson’s 10 principles of Heuristics Evaluation, I came up with my concept and ideas for enhancing the Zara Website.

Firstly, I redesigned the landing page of the Zara Website, primarily targeting the navigation bar by adding a white background behind it and having it stick to the top while scrolling to avoid the text layering on top of images, I also relocated the search bar to the middle and added icons the search bar and shopping bag for better visibility.

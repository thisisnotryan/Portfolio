Z Day Zero Mobile App

About Project

Z Day Zero is a fictional game produced by me for my User Experience Design Module assignments. The game is the main focus across my three assignments with the first being a Logo Design for the game, second being a game promo website for the game, and the third being a forum and game guide app for the game.
For the Z Day Zero Mobile App, it mainly focuses on game guides with information, tips and tricks about the game's characters, and zombies. Along with a media gallery that showcases official trailers, gameplay videos and concept art. Most importantly, it has a community forum where players can come together to share experiences, discuss strategies and give feedback to the game developers.

Z Day Zero Website

About Project

Z Day Zero is a fictional game produced by me for my User Experience Design Module assignments. The game is the main focus across my three assignments with the first being a Logo Design for the game, second being a game promo website for the game, and the third being a forum and game guide app for the game.
For the game promo website, it mainly focuses on catching the viewer's attention with a landing page consisting of the countdown timer to the game's official release followed by showcasing the game's official game trailer and some brief information of the game. Next, it shows the recommended PC Specs for the users and after that we further intrigue the user by letting interact with website to know more about the types of zombies presented in the game. At this point, the viewer is under a good impression of the game and we suggest a pre-order purchase of the game to the viewers and show them the available platforms and prices of the game. Last but not least, We ended off the website with available merchandise for purchase to go along with the game.

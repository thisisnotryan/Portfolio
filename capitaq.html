About Project

CapitaQ is a queueing app designed for restaurants in CapitaLand, offering customers the ability to join a virtual queue while on the go, eliminating the need for physical waiting.

This project is a group project of 5 members and the primary objective that we as a group want to achieve is to develop a queuing app for restuarants in specific malls to enhance the overall dining experience for consumers by offering a more convenient and efficient method of managing wait times. What differs this app from reality is that this app allows customers to join a virtual queue as they are en route to the restuarant, or anywhere on the go, thus eliminating the need to physically wait in line.

What CapitaQ aims to achieve
- Minimize the inconvenience of long wait times for customers
- Allow consumers to better manage their time while navigating the mall
- Enhance operational efficiency for restaurants by optimizing the flow of customers
- Contribute to increased engagement and foot traffic across mall establishments

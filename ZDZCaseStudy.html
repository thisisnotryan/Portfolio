Introduction

Z DAY ZERO is a fictional horror zombie first-person shooter game created as part of my project work.
This project was divided into three key phases:
1. Logo Creation – Designing the official branding for Z DAY ZERO.
2. Promotional Website – Developing a website to market the game as an upcoming release, allowing potential players to pre-order.
3. Z DAY ZERO APP – Creating the ultimate companion app to help players navigate the game's chaotic world. Whether you're a seasoned survivor or a new recruit, the app enhances the gaming experience and keeps players connected with the Z DAY ZERO community.

Logo Creation

Starting with the Logo concept, I want to present the logo's typography in a bold, distressed way to convey the harsh, survivalist theme of the game and as for the imagery elements, I played around with graphics that symbolizes the onset of the outbreak, hence I started sketching various logo designs that represent things such as a zombie's hand, a skull, the typical zombie bio-hazard sign, even weapons such as a gun or something melee-related to capture the user's attention and to grasp the strong identity of the game.


Ultimately, 1 logo stood out the most that I felt has the most potential for a digital rendition and that is the very first zombie-hand that I drew



This logo was decided on because I felt that the chopped-off zombie's hand has all the information and detail needed to immediately know what the game "Z DAY ZERO" is about. Upon first glance, it should be known to players that the game is of the horror genre and is regarding zombies.
As for the font choice, the Edo SZ font was choses as I love how the font looks with the brush-like writing style, it gives off the feeling of the font is being written with blood which fits the game's theme perfectly. As for the sub logo, I played around with the spacing sizing of each individual letter to create a jammed/claustrophobic feeling to give off the aggressive touch.

Promotional Website

For the promotional website, I started doing my research and to gather various moodboard ideas for inspiration and reference, after I found a few potential designs that caught my eye, I drew and sketch out the wireframe layout I wanted for my promotional website on paper.


For the layout design, firstly I want the promotional website to start with a eye-ctaching landing page consisting of the countdown timer to the game's official release.

After catching the user’s attention with the countdown to game’s release, present them with the game’s official game trailer and some brief information of the game

After watching the game trailer, show the recommended PC Specs for the user as at this point, we have already caught the user’s attention to the game

After knowing the PC Specs, intrigue the user more by letting them interact with the website to know more about the types of zombies presented in the game

After the user is under good impression of the game, at this point we will suggest they make a purchase of the game and show the available platforms and prices of the game

Finally, end off the website with available merchandise to go with the purchase of the game


Last but not least, I added the color scheme and design in and this is how the final design for the promotional website looked!


Z DAY ZERO APP

The Z DAY ZERO APP mainly consist of 6 core features for players and users to interact with, these features are:
1. Game News & Updates feature
2. Zombie Compendium feature
3. Character Profiles feature
4. Tips & Tricks feature
5. Community Forum feature
6. Media Gallery feature


Game News & Updates

The Game News & Updates feature helps users & players stay informed with the latest news,updates, and patch notes.
It is vital to keep playeds informed and engaged with regular updates as the gaming lanscape is always changing with new contents, balance adjustments, bug fixes, etc. Via this feature, players can also be notified about special upcoming events such as specific in-game events, live QNA sessions with the development team, and special appearances of Youtubers/Streamers as they play the game.

Zombie Compendium

The Zombie Compendium allows players to discover and learn detailed information about the various types of zombies they would face, including their strenghts, weaknesses, and best tactics for defeating them.
Zombies are a critical component of the Z DAY ZERO game. Therefore it is crucial that we provide players with detailed information about the various types of zombies they will encounter in the game. Doing so will allow players to understand the unique abilities, strengths, and weaknesses of each zombie, hence allowing players to develop effective strategies and counteract each threat and be more prepared to face the apocalypse in Z DAY ZERO.

Character Profiles

The Character Profile feature allows players to dive deep into the lore and abilities of the game's four unique characters, by doing so we provide players with a more immersive gaming experience. Providing detailed backgrounds of each character's backstory adds depth to the game, making the characters more relatable as players play as them in Z DAY ZERO.
Each character’s unique abilities are also provided to help players grasp how to effectively use them in different scenarios, enhancing their strategic and overall gaming experience.

Tips & Tricks

The Tips & Tricks feature provides players valuable insights and strategies to improve their gameplay, regardless if a player is new to the game or has survived long hours in the apocalypse, knowing some tips & tricks will help players improve their skills and better understand the game's mechanics, allowing them to become more proficient and enjoy the game to its fullest.
This feature also provide critical tips on each of the characters and zombies' strengths and weaknesses as well as general tips for overall gameplay and multiplayer experience

Community Forum

The Community Forum allows players to engage with each other. They can ask questions, share experiences, and discuss their strategies with other survivors.
The forum encourages players to actively participate in discussions, share their experiences, and contribute to the game’s community. It is also a direct channel for players to give feedback to the developers, sharing their thoughts on recent updates, features, or any bugs found in the game so that the developers can gather these valuable insights and suggestions from the community.

Media Gallery

The Media Gallery feature provides players the opportunity to explore a rich collection of official trailers, gameplay videos, and concept art.
It is also a platform to highlight media that is generated content from players such as fan art, screenshots of hilarious moments of the game, and videos showcasing the creativity and passion of the Z DAY ZERO community.
Through the media gallery, developers are also able to showcase the concept art and design iterations hence allowing players to appreciate the artistic journey and evolution of the game’s visual.

Reflection

Throughout these projects, I gained several key takeaways that have greatly influenced my approach to design and development.
Firstly, I realized that having a solid understanding of a project beforehand significantly speeds up the workflow and reduces the workload. Taking the time to conduct proper research, gather references, and create mood boards is an essential step that should never be overlooked.
Secondly, I learned the importance of investing time and effort into initial sketches and wireframes. Iterating at this stage is much easier and more efficient compared to making major changes later in the process.
Lastly, although I managed to complete and submit all my projects on time, there were moments when I felt rushed. It made me wonder—if I had started earlier, would my work have been even better? This experience reinforced the importance of early planning and time management to ensure a smoother process and higher-quality results.
